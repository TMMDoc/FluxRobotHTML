Manifold

Manifold

  • Model: SMC SS5Y3-10F1-05DS-C6

Solenoid Configuration:

  • 5/3 closed-center valve.

    • Features:

  • 5 stations.

  • 10 side-faced ports.

  • Connected via 25-pin D-sub connector.

    • Indicators:

  • Red light for A port.

  • Green light for B port.

    • Port Configuration (connected to the robot’s I/O in the following order, as shown in (Pic 3.1)):

  • 1A – Push Backward

  • 1B – Push Forward

  • 2A – Gripper Close

  • 2B – Gripper Open

  • 3A – Pallet Dispenser Enable

  • 3B – Gripper Enable

  • 4A – Pallet Dispenser Down

  • 4B – Pallet Dispenser Up

  • 5A – Not connected

  • 5B – Not connected

Vacuum Ejector on the teach pendant

Vacuum Gripper on ROBOT:

Step 1: We have to enable the inlet air to the vacuum ejector mounted at the J3 of robot, do it navigate to IO>>[TYPE]>>Digital>>OUT and turn ON DO[501]-GRIP_ENB and turn OFF DO[502]-PDISP_ENB

DO 501 and 502

STEP 2: The vacuum ejector has a always ON configuration for suction i.e, the suction is turned ON when the Digital signal goes OFF . *DO[505] VAC_GRIP_ON is the DO for controlling vacuum suction at the robot EOAT. When DO[505] is OFF then suction is turned ON and vice versa

DO[505]- is tuned OFF to turn suction ON at EOAT

When

When DO[501]-is disabled RED lED truns off

and if you turn ON DO[502] – then Green LED turns ON indicating inlet air to vacuum ejector for partDispenser unit is ON
